About This Wiki

This wiki provides everything needed to build a 30 ft geodesic dome:

  • Interactive 3D previews — inspect strut types, hub positions, and dome geometry in real time
  • Downloadable CAD files — STL for 3D printing, OBJ for CAD import, DXF for CNC/laser hub plates
  • Exact chord factors — from the Berkeley Math Circle paper by Tom Davis (2004)
  • Material estimates — Home Depot 2026 pricing with shopping lists
  • Construction guidance — assembly times, crew sizes, tool requirements
